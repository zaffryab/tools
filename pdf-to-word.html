Convert PDF to Word Online for Free

Our PDF to Word converter is a powerful online tool that allows you to transform your PDF documents into fully editable Microsoft Word files. Whether you need to edit a contract, modify a report, or extract text from a scanned document, our converter makes the process simple and efficient.

Why Use Our PDF to Word Converter?

  • High Accuracy: Preserves original formatting, fonts, and layout
  • OCR Technology: Convert scanned PDFs to editable text
  • No Registration: Free to use with no sign-up required
  • Secure Processing: Files are deleted after conversion
  • Works on Any Device: Compatible with Windows, Mac, and mobile

How to Convert PDF to Word:

  1. Click "Upload PDF File" or drag and drop your PDF
  2. Select your conversion options (quality, OCR, etc.)
  3. Click "Convert to Word" button
  4. Download your editable Word document

Supported Word Formats:

Our converter supports output in both .docx (Microsoft Word 2007 and later) and .doc (compatible with older versions of Word) formats. You can choose your preferred format before conversion.
